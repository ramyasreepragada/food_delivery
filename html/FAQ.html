Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What makes FeedMore different from other food delivery services? A: FeedMore stands out by not only offering fast and efficient food delivery but also by integrating a unique charitable initiative. Every order contributes to our mission of reducing food waste and supporting charitable causes.

Q: How can I donate excess food through FeedMore? A: If you're a restaurant or individual with excess food, FeedMore makes it easy to donate. Simply sign up as a donor, provide details about the surplus food available, and our platform will connect you with local charitable organizations or orphanages ready to receive the donation.

Q: Is FeedMore available in my area? A: We are continually expanding our reach. To check if FeedMore is available in your area, download the app and enter your location details. We are committed to serving as many communities as possible.
